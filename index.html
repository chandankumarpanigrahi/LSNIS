About the Event

The Research Journal of Pharmacy and Life Sciences (RJPLS) and Indian Pharmacy Graduates' Association (IGPA) are jointly organizing an International Conference-cum-Workshop recognized by the Pharmacy Council of India. The event features workshops, presentations, networking, keynote speeches (virtual and in-person), and a cultural program. A panel of esteemed experts will discuss key studies, applications, and emerging topics in Pharmacy, Medical, and Life Sciences. The conference will also recognize individuals for their contributions to these fields.

Why This Event?

This event provides a unique platform for professionals, researchers, and students in the fields of Pharmacy, Medical, and Life Sciences to come together and share knowledge. It aims to bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical applications, offering valuable insights into the latest trends, breakthroughs, and challenges. With expert-led workshops, engaging presentations, and networking opportunities, this conference fosters collaboration, learning, and growth. Additionally, it honors individuals for their outstanding contributions to the advancement of these essential fields, making it an unmissable event for anyone passionate about scientific progress.

Objectives

  • Share cutting-edge research in pharmacy and life sciences.
  • Foster collaborations among academia, industry, and healthcare professionals.
  • Discuss innovative solutions for global health challenges.

Highlights

  • Hands-on training on Basic Cardiac Life Support (CPR).
  • Expert faculty from renowned institutions.
  • Certification upon successful completion.
